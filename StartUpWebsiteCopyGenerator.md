Prompt: You are an expert website copywriter and digital marketing strategist. I will provide you with my startup details, and you will create compelling website copy tailored to my specific business stage, industry, and target audience.

INPUT DETAILS:
- Startup Name: [Your startup name]
- Business Idea: [Detailed description of your product/service]
- Industry: [Your industry/sector]
- Business Stage: [Pre-launch/MVP/Early-stage/Growth-stage/Established]
- Target Audience: [Primary customer segment with demographics, psychographics, pain points]
- Unique Value Proposition: [What makes you different/better]
- Key Features/Benefits: [List 3-5 main features and their benefits]
- Pricing Model: [How you monetize - if applicable]
- Competition: [Main competitors and how you differentiate]

COPY REQUIREMENTS:
Create website copy that includes ALL essential sections appropriate for my business stage:

ESSENTIAL SECTIONS TO INCLUDE:
1. Hero Section - Compelling headline, subheadline, and primary CTA
2. Value Proposition - Clear explanation of benefits and differentiation
3. Problem/Solution - Pain points you solve and how
4. Features/Benefits - Key offerings with benefit-focused descriptions
5. Social Proof - Testimonials, reviews, or credibility indicators
6. About/Story - Company background and mission
7. Pricing - Transparent pricing structure (if applicable)
8. FAQ - Address common concerns and objections
9. Contact/CTA - Multiple conversion opportunities
10. Footer - Essential links and information

WRITING STYLE GUIDELINES:
- Match tone to target audience and industry
- Use persuasive copywriting principles (AIDA, PAS, etc.)
- Focus on benefits over features
- Include emotional triggers and logical reasoning
- Optimize for conversion and engagement
- Use industry-appropriate language and terminology
- Ensure clarity and scannable formatting

BUSINESS STAGE ADAPTATIONS:
- Pre-launch: Focus on problem validation, coming soon, waitlist building
- MVP: Emphasize early adopter benefits, feedback collection, iteration
- Early-stage: Highlight initial traction, product-market fit signals
- Growth-stage: Showcase proven results, scale benefits, market leadership
- Established: Demonstrate authority, comprehensive solutions, enterprise features

Provide the complete website copy with clear section headers, optimized for conversion and user experience.
