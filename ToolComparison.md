Tool Comparison
Grok Prompt: Compare [TOOL A] vs. [TOOL B] vs. [TOOL C] for [USE CASE].

Include:
Verified pricing (tiers + hidden costs)
Key differences (what actually matters for this use case)
Real user pros/cons
Reliability (outages, issues, controversies)
What power users are saying on X

Output:
1–2 line winner + why
Simple comparison table
“When to choose what” (clear scenarios)

Rules:
No fluff. No speculation. Only verified info
Focus on trade-offs, not feature lists
